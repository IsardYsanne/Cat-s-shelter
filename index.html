animal shelter

The Animal Shelter focuses on accessible spay and neuter programs in an effort to beat animal population at its core. Creative adoption efforts and dozens of programs provide support for marginalized animals and work to place homeless animals into loving families. Come visit our facilities to experience how Animal Shelter cares for its animals or take a virtual tour instead. The Shelter is not just a place to adopt an animal or to find a missing one, but a place to visit over and over again. This is your Shelter, built by this community – a place to find joy with the animals.
Paulie, sunny cat

Paulie

Paulie (TX) is a neutered male Domestic Short Hair orange tabby cat rescued in Texas. We are not sure sure yet of Paulie's age, but he is a young cat. Paulie is good with people and other cats. This poor guy was found with an embedded collar which was obviously put on him when he was a kitten and never replaced as he grew. Our veterinary partner surgically removed the collar and now Paulie is on the road to his forever home and forever happiness!

Dark Charlotte

Once comfortable, Charlotte is an avid explorer and is especially happy to investigate if food is involved. Keep some liver snacks on hand and Charlotte will provide the cuddles. She is always sure to finish her meals and never complains about taking medicine, especially if it’s followed by some behind the ear scratches. Charlotte has a ton of love to give and just needs a human to show her love back! This cat would prefer to be adopted alone and be the only cat in the home.
